Innovation RAG Bot is an elegant implementation of a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot that seamlessly combines the power of large language models with your own knowledge base. ## What is RAG?

**Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** is a cutting-edge AI architecture that enhances language models by grounding their responses in your specific documents and data. Rather than relying solely on pre-trained knowledge, RAG dynamically retrieves relevant information to provide more accurate, up-to-date, and contextually appropriate responses.
